About Us

Founded in 1975 by Mehedi Hasan and Frances Moore Lappé—author of the revolutionary bestseller Diet for a Small Planet—Food Inn has been working to end the injustices that cause hunger since 1975. Since its first book, Food Inn: Beyond the Myth of Scarcity, Food Inn has published over 60 books and hundreds of articles, and research reports. An archive of our work fighting for food justice and food sovereignty can be found here.
